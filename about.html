About Us

The TOU TOD Live Tariff Price Monitor is a developed application designed to help households better manage their energy use by predicting daily electricity rates based on historical TOU tariff information. Real-time prices are depicted on the platform, providing users with guidance on the amounts of energy that should be consumed.

Some of the key features include the depiction of real-time prices, the recommended schedule based on the predicted tariff, notifications to individuals about energy savings, and the selection of suitable appliances for use during specific periods. Automated emails notify users about upcoming price changes and recommend which appliances to use at particular times of the day.

This approach not only helps users save on electricity costs by allowing them to adjust their usage according to predicted rates but also encourages the adoption of renewable energy. The TOU TOD Live Tariff Price Monitor makes energy management simpler for users and enables them to align their schedules with an efficient and environmentally friendly energy system.
